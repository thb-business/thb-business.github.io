Winterfest

By Sarah Syed

Winter Fest 2025: A Night of Fun and Festivities! Looking for something exciting to do? Want a chance to enjoy games, food, and fun with your friends? Then get ready—Winter Fest is coming! On November 28th, just before the winter holidays, the whole school will be celebrating one of the most exciting events of the year. Winter Fest is packed with games, raffle tickets, delicious food, and so much more. It’s your chance to play, win prizes, and enjoy a great evening with friends and classmates. So mark the date, bring your best festive spirit, and get ready for a night you won’t forget. Winter Fest only happens once a year—don’t miss it!
